We presented PhysAgent, which generates physically grounded SDevice files by combining {\color{red}an ontology built from the Sentaurus TCAD guide} with literature-driven domain knowledge, enabling general-purpose LLMs to support expert-level TCAD modeling without synthetic training data. On a Si nMOSFET benchmark, PhysAgent achieves {\color{red}10/10 pass} at 81\% {\color{red}mention rate}, versus {\color{red}0/10 pass} for both raw-text and no-context baselines.
