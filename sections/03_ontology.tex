\subsection{Presentation type}\label{AA}
SISPAD 2026 will be held in-person and accepts oral and poster presentations.

Final determination of the presentation type will be made by the Technical Program Committee.

\subsection{Requirements}
\begin{itemize}
\item Deadline for submission: https://sispad2026.jp/
\item All abstracts must be written in English.
\item Please prepare the abstract in two pages (A4 portrait, including tables and figures), and the file size must be less than 10 MB.
\item A font size between 10 and 12 points is suggested.
\item Abstracts sent by e-mail or postal mail will NOT be considered. Please submit your abstracts using our online submission portal at https://sispad2026.jp/.
\item You can NOT use two-byte fonts like Japanese, Korean, Chinese characters or single-byte Asian fonts such as MS Gothic, MS Mincho, Ryumin, or Batang.
\end{itemize}
