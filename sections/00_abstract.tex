As semiconductor scaling reaches its limits, Technology Computer-Aided Design (TCAD) simulation becomes essential for Design-Technology Co-Optimization (DTCO). However, the core challenge lies in configuring SDevice---the physics-and-solver specification stage of Sentaurus TCAD---rather than in device structure generation. We propose PhysAgent, which builds a Sentaurus TCAD guide-based ontology and integrates literature-driven domain knowledge to generate physically grounded SDevice files without relying on synthetic training data.
