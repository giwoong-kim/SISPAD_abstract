As semiconductor scaling reaches its limits, Technology Computer-Aided Design (TCAD) simulation becomes essential for Design-Technology Co-Optimization (DTCO)~{\color{red}\cite{dtco}}. However, the core challenge lies in configuring SDevice---the physics-and-solver specification stage of {\color{red}Synopsys} Sentaurus TCAD~{\color{red}\cite{synopsys_tcad}}---rather than in device structure generation. We propose PhysAgent, which {\color{red}builds an ontology from the Sentaurus TCAD guide, augmented with literature-driven domain knowledge}, to generate physically grounded SDevice files without relying on synthetic training data.
