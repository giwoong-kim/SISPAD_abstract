As the limits of semiconductor scaling become increasingly apparent, the importance of DTCO and TCAD continues to grow. However, the core challenge of TCAD automation lies not in simple structure generation, but in supporting physically reliable simulation setup and modeling. In particular, Sdevice parameter files cannot be handled as a mere code generation task, since they must reflect physical models, material properties, and constraints among parameters at the same time. Moreover, modeling requires interpretation of the complex interactions among multiple parameters, which calls for automation equipped with physical knowledge. Therefore, this work proposes PhysAgent, which structures TCAD knowledge into an ontology to support expert-level modeling.
